About Tshwanelo Placements

Tshwanelo Placements is a people-centered recruitment and staffing agency dedicated to connecting reliable talent with meaningful employment across South Africa. We specialise in domestic, hospitality, and administrative placements while offering bookkeeping and Sage training services.

Our Promise

We charge affordable, once-off placement fees. No hidden costs. We take time to understand both employers and candidates to ensure long-term, respectful matches.

  • Integrity & Professionalism in every placement
  • Transparent pricing — once-off fees
  • Empowerment through training & mentorship

Origin

Born from witnessing skilled people struggle to find stable work — Tshwanelo bridges the gap between job seekers (especially women) and trustworthy employers.

What Makes Us Different

We build relationships, mentor job seekers, and focus on ethical, long-term placements — not quick hires.

Why Come To Us

Clients choose us for our hands-on support, personal approach and a reputation for honest, effective matching. We prioritise safety, fit and ongoing satisfaction.

Ideal Client

Working professionals or families (aged 30–55), urban/suburban, middle to upper income, who value reliability and a smooth recruitment process.

Five Things We Stand For

  • Empowering job seekers with fair opportunity
  • Delivering trustworthy and transparent services
  • Personalised placements matching both sides
  • Supporting women & youth access to sustainable work
  • Upholding strong ethical standards
