About Tshwanelo Placements Pty Ltd

We are a people-centered recruitment and staffing agency dedicated to connecting reliable talent with meaningful employment opportunities across South Africa. We specialize in domestic, hospitality, and administrative placements, ensuring professionalism, trust, and long-term partnerships.

Our Story

Tshwanelo Placements was born from witnessing capable individuals, especially women, struggle to find stable jobs despite their skills. We exist to bridge that gap — offering fair, honest, and supportive placement solutions.

Our ideal clients are professional households and businesses seeking reliable and skilled staff who value peace of mind, trust, and respectful engagement. Our affordable, once-off placement fees make the recruitment process transparent, stress-free, and effective.

We focus not just on filling roles, but building relationships. Our approach is personal, empathetic, and guided by integrity. We provide mentorship and guidance to job seekers, ensuring that every placement is meaningful and successful.
